Improving HIS Processes

    • Flexible standard
    • Local adaptation of measurement categories
  • Data Collection
    • Data hybridization
    • Actionable map of population for public health actors
  • Data Analysis
    • Differentiate data problems from signal
    • Orient supervision of health system

Aim 3 : Data Monitoring

Open Results Based Funding in Bénin

  1. Facilities report monthly on contracted activities
  2. Data is validated by District officers
  3. Payment is made based on validated data

Data

Research question

How to optimize supervision strategy to lower the costs and keep data quality and service quality in check ?

Data Monitoring Questions

  1. How do you find signal in low quality data ?
  2. How do you monitor indicators with unclear denominators ?
  3. What is the optimal amount of data you need to do these ?

General Approach

  1. Define expected distribution for indicator
  2. Define a threshold based on desirable detection characteristics
  3. Compare collected data to expected distrubtion
  4. Classify out of control situations

Available Methods

  1. Syndromic surveillance ?
    1. Based on CUSUM methods
    2. Cumulative error of predicted value
  2. Health System regulation / benchmarking
    1. Bayesian Hierarchical Modeling
    2. Inference on posterior distribution of random effects
  3. Industrial Statistical Process Control / Profile Monitoring
    1. Monitoring of relationship between indicators
    2. Complex threshold definition

Methods

  1. Fit and calibrate different monitoring models + mixtures
  2. Classify spotted divergence into data problems and non data problems
  3. Simulate different supervision strategies and compare detection performances
